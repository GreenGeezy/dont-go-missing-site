FAQ

Answers before you head out.

What is Don't Go Missing?

Don't Go Missing is a premium iOS wilderness safety app that helps users create a Safe Journey before heading into remote places.

Is Don't Go Missing an emergency service?

No. It is a safety planning app and is not a replacement for emergency services.

Does the app guarantee rescue?

No. It helps you prepare and share your plan, but it cannot guarantee rescue, response, connectivity, or location accuracy.

Who is the app for?

It is designed for hikers, solo hikers, hunters, anglers, campers, backcountry drivers, and off-grid outdoorsmen.

Does the app work offline?

The app is designed around offline-first safety planning. Some actions still depend on your device, settings, location services, and available signal.

Does the app use my location?

Location may be used to show maps, plan journeys, support check-ins, and prepare SOS messages when location is available and permissions allow.

How are subscriptions handled?

Subscriptions are handled through Apple. You can manage or cancel them in Apple subscriptions.

How do I contact support or request data deletion?

Use the contact page or email support@dontgomissing.com.
